✦ Our origins

Born in the Colorado mountains.

Two Leaves and a Bud started in 2004 with one goal: a genuinely great cup of tea, sourced honestly and made without pretense. Two decades on, that same standard still decides what makes it into a sachet — and what doesn't.

Quality, without pretense

Whole leaf, hand-plucked, and judged by the cup — not the marketing.

Care for people & planet

Compostable packaging wherever we can, and fair terms for growers.

Transparent sourcing

We can trace nearly every leaf back to the garden it grew in.
